More About Me


Here are some other things you should know.


Experience

Coding has only come into my life relatively recently. After graduating from a BA in Linguistics and TESOL and hearing about opportunities in the tech sector, I began to teach myself to code, and quickly discovered how awesome it feels to code something that actually does something cool. In order to hone my skills and prepare myself for a career in the industry, I enrolled to study a Master of Software Development, and now I work as a developer on Koha (an open-source library system) at Catalyst IT.


Purpose

I quickly realised as I taught myself different programming concepts and languages, that I had stumbled upon an area of technology that not only excited me, but also presented me an avenue through which to fulfil my purpose: to create things that might in some way have an impact on the people around me, and those in the wider world. This is what I hope to do going forward in my career, starting with Koha.


Interests

My background and previous degree are a great indicator of my interests, as linguistics remains a major passion and source of enthusiasm in my life. Ideally, one day I'd love to be able to use my proficiencies in the field of linguistics in conjunction with my active interest in coding to help solve larger communicative problems.
